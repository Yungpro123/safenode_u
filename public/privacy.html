SafeNode — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SafeNode. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account details: name, email, password, and country.
  • Transaction data: amount, type (deposit, withdrawal, escrow), and payment reference.
  • Technical data: browser type, device information, and IP address (for security logs).
  • Optional info: If you verify your identity (KYC), we may collect ID and selfie images.

3. How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your SafeNode account.
  • Process payments, escrow releases, and withdrawals.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or suspicious activity.
  • Send you important updates about your account and transactions.
  • Improve SafeNode and provide better customer support.

4. Cookies

We use small text files called cookies to remember your login session and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not work properly without them.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with trusted third parties when necessary:

  • Payment providers (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave, Opay) to process your transactions.
  • Email providers (e.g., Gmail or SendGrid) to send account or verification emails.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or court order.

6. Data storage & security

We store data on secure servers and use encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your personal information. However, no system is 100% secure. By using SafeNode, you accept the limited risk inherent in internet-based services.

7. How long we keep data

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as required by law. When your account is deleted, we remove or anonymize personal data unless it’s needed for legal or accounting purposes.

8. Your rights

  • You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • You can ask us to correct or delete your data (unless we must keep it for legal reasons).
  • You can unsubscribe from emails anytime.

To make a request, email us at support@safenode.com.

9. Third-party links

SafeNode may link to external websites (like payment partners). We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

10. Children’s privacy

SafeNode is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we find such data, it will be deleted immediately.

11. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. The latest version will always be posted here with the effective date at the top.

12. Contact us

If you have any questions, complaints, or data requests, please contact:

Email: support@safenode.com
