About SafeNode

SafeNode is a secure escrow platform that protects both buyers and sellers by holding funds until the transaction is completed successfully. Our mission is to make online transactions safer, faster, and more transparent.

  • Escrow protection for both parties
  • Supports NGN and USDT payments
  • Fast resolution in case of disputes
  • Transparent contract and transaction history

Getting Started

  • Create and verify your SafeNode account.
  • Set up your NGN or USDT wallet.
  • Fund your wallet using supported payment methods.
  • Start creating or joining contracts immediately.

If you're new to escrow, SafeNode guides you step-by-step.

Funding Your Wallet

  • NGN deposits supported through Paystack.
  • USDT deposits supported on BNB Smart Chain.
  • Withdrawals available anytime with minimal delay.
  • All transactions are monitored for security.

Creating Contracts

  • Choose contract type: product, service, digital goods, or custom.
  • Set deadlines, expectations, and payment responsibility.
  • Buyer deposits funds which are locked until completion.
  • Seller receives funds only after approval.

Disputes & Resolution

If an issue arises, SafeNode offers a fair, structured process:

  • Open a dispute within the contract page.
  • Upload evidence such as screenshots, receipts, or messages.
  • SafeNode reviews materials from both sides.
  • A final decision is made based on evidence and policy.

Security & Passwords

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account.
  • Enable two-step verification when available.
  • Never share credentials with anyone.
  • Reset forgotten passwords instantly via your email.

Fees & Transactions

SafeNode charges a 6 percent service fee per escrow transaction. The fee may be assigned to the buyer, seller, or split evenly. No hidden charges, no additional deductions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my transaction pending?

Transactions may take a few minutes to process depending on network congestion or payment provider delays. Please wait up to 15 minutes before contacting support.

Can I cancel a contract after funding it?

Yes, contracts can be canceled if both buyer and seller agree. If a dispute exists, cancellation will follow SafeNode's resolution policies.

How long do withdrawals take?

NGN withdrawals through Paystack usually process within minutes. USDT withdrawals depend on BNB Smart Chain network speed.

Is SafeNode available outside Nigeria?

Yes, anyone can use SafeNode for USDT-based transactions. NGN payments are currently limited to supported regions.

What happens if either party fails to respond?

SafeNode may manually review the transaction and release funds based on available evidence and platform policies.



Troubleshooting

I deposited USDT, but it's not showing.

Confirm that you sent USDT on the correct network (BNB Smart Chain). Sending funds on the wrong chain can result in permanent loss.

I can't log into my account.

Use the "Forgot Password" option or contact support if you no longer have access to your email.

The other party isn't responding.

Open a dispute within the contract. SafeNode will take over and review the matter.
