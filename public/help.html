About SafeNode

SafeNode is a secure escrow payment system that protects buyers and sellers during online transactions. Funds are held safely until both sides are satisfied with the outcome.

Getting Started

  • Register your account and verify your email.
  • Access your SafeNode wallet to deposit or withdraw funds.
  • Supported methods: Paystack (NGN) and USDT on BNB network.

Creating Contracts

  • Go to your dashboard and click “Create Contract”.
  • Fill in contract details and choose who pays the service charge.
  • Buyer funds the contract; SafeNode holds funds securely.

Disputes & Resolution

If issues arise, open a dispute from your contract page. Provide evidence and messages — our team reviews and ensures a fair outcome.

Security & Passwords

  • Use a strong password and never share it with anyone.
  • SafeNode will never ask for your credentials.
  • Reset passwords through the “Forgot Password” option.

Fees & Transactions

SafeNode charges a 6% service fee per escrow transaction, which can be paid by the buyer, seller, or split evenly. No hidden costs or extra deductions.

Contact & Support

  • Live chat: Available in your dashboard
  • Response time: Within 24 hours (Mon–Fri)
